Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: I have placed my order what next?
Answer

Sit back and relax! Once your order is confirmed, we will share the order id and tracking details via E-mail/SMS.

Q: What can I return?
Answer

You may request returns for most items you buy from us within the return window, except those that are explicitly identified as not returnable.

Q: When will I get my refund?
Answer

Refunds are processed after we receive the item. Once received, refund times depend on the payment method selected. Typically it comes within 3-4 business days.

Q: What if I miss my order delivery?
Answer

Do not worry, the delivery agent will try to deliver again on the next working day. You can also call the delivery agent to get the latest delivery update.
